2

The header on the left displays the storyboard title and essential tools:

• Edit: Modify your storyboard.

• Duplicate: Create a copy.

• Share: Collaborate with others.

• Lock: Prevent edits.

• Archive: Organize completed storyboards.

3

The right side of the header provides quick access to important updates and management tools:

• Bell icon: Stay updated with notifications about your storyboards and activities.

• Dashboard icon: Manage all your storyboards, invite members, and review archived content.

4

Click the User icon to reveal three tabs:

• Storyboards: Manage all storyboards.

• Settings: Customize appearance and functionality.

• Account: Adjust account preferences.

Each tab provide with additional tools and options to control appearance and to manage the account.

5

Control how scenes are displayed:

• Tile Mode: View scenes as a grid.

• List Mode: See scene details in a vertical list.

• Slide Mode: Display one scene at a time.

Refine these options further in Settings.

6

Hover over any image to reveal the Three-dot menu for quick actions:

• Replace the image.

• Add or edit the description.

• Reorder, Duplicate or Delete scenes.

Click directly on a scene image to open a Comment view for team collaboration.

7

The Add Scene button, offers flexible options:

• Add Scene: Will immediately add a single scene.

• Dropdown options:

– Import Images: To upload multiple scenes.

– Sorting: Sort by file name, or change order.

This flexibility ensures you can easily add individual scenes or organize bulk uploads with precision.
