Selected Work

  
Signals (2024) — Research Integrity Platform

  
Academic institutions use these dashboards to monitor research integrity. Three weeks of analytics told us researchers were spending 60% of their time scrolling, not analyzing. We switched to tabbed navigation with sticky metrics and dynamic filtering. Information retrieval got 60% faster, WCAG compliance hit 100%, scrolling dropped 5x. Full case study.


  
Anylyze (2024) — Analytics Data Platform

  
Users didn't trust the data. That was the core problem. I rebuilt the visualization layer with a three-tiered typography system and five component states so you could always tell what the data was doing. Tasks got 38% faster, errors dropped 71%, and user confidence hit 9.4/10. Full case study.


  
LiveU (2024) — Signa Design System

  
120+ components for a global live video broadcasting platform. Atomic design, dual-typeface system (Plus Jakarta Sans + Inter), 8px grid. The system now runs across multiple products and regions. Design velocity went up 60% and cross-platform consistency hit 100%. Full case study.


  
TUIASI (2023) — University Website Redesign

  
The university's website was a decade old, slow, and hemorrhaging prospective students. We had four weeks. Stripped the IA down, killed the bloat, optimized everything. That year they got +4,200 new students (a record), pages got 91% smaller, loading went 10x faster. Full case study.


  
ResNet AI (2023) — Hospitality Design System

  
1,300+ scattered design variants. No documentation, no governance, no consistency. I consolidated the whole thing into a token-based system with proper docs and component governance. 60% fewer components, 40% faster handoff, 100% accessibility compliance. Full case study.


  
Socyal (2023) — HR Mobile Platform

  
Took an HR tool from concept to an investor-ready standalone mobile product in 5 months. Designed the complete mobile experience — onboarding flows, performance management, feedback loops, and team dynamics dashboards. Launched as #3 Product of the Day on Product Hunt. Worked on this project for 6+ years alongside the founding team. Full case study.


  
App4Home (2024) — Smart Home IoT App

  
The existing smart home app was cluttered and confusing. I redesigned it with a time-aware dashboard that adapts to daily routines, room-based navigation for grouping devices, and AI-powered recommendations. Visual clutter dropped 40%, onboarding got 2x faster. Full case study.


  
CyberGhost VPN (2018) — Consumer Privacy App

  
The interface was built by engineers, for engineers. I made it work for everyone else. Dark-mode-first, a slide-to-connect interaction that felt physical, and a 3-step onboarding you could finish in 30 seconds. Full case study.


  
CognitiveSEO (2017) — SEO Dashboard

  
Feature-heavy dashboard where power users thrived and everyone else drowned. I broke it into three modules with progressive disclosure. Power users kept everything; marketers could finally navigate without a manual. Tasks got 40% faster, retention went up 30%, cognitive load dropped 45%. Full case study.


  
Big5 American Diner (2020) — Restaurant Brand Identity

  
Five founding recipes, one brand. Mid-century American diner aesthetic across logo, menus, packaging, signage, and digital. Big5 American Diner became #1 Hospitality Brand in Iasi within its first year. No paid ads, purely organic. Full case study.


  
Darnic for Education (2024) — NGO Campaign

  
"Generosity builds futures." A Christmas gift-giving campaign for underserved children in rural Romania. I designed the logo, campaign materials, social templates, and volunteer kits. Kit production went 3x faster once everything was templated and consistent. Full case study.


  
Crafting Social Stories (2024) — Workshop Brand

  
Educational workshops for children in rural Romania. The brand had to work for two very different audiences: kids (playful) and corporate donors (credible). Getting that balance right drove volunteer sign-ups and donor engagement. Full case study.
